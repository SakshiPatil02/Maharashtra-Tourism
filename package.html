Hill Stations

Mahabaleshwar

The gorgeous colonial vibe of the hill station interspersed with the cold breeze of the Sahyadris certainly make it a great place for a vacation.

Lonavala

Nestled in the heart of the Sahyadri range is the beautiful hill station in Maharashtra. Lonavala is a paradise for all kinds of tourists flocking over to enjoy the cold breeze at the mountain top in Maharashtra.

Igatpuri

The place does come with a great vibe thanks to the sparkling lakes, invigorating valleys, vintage forts and more. It is one of the best hill stations in Maharashtra.

Forts

Raigad Fort

Considered as one of the prides of the Marathas, the fort comes with a rich history. And 15 different names, each given to it by different rulers of Raigad.

Sindhu Garh Fort

Sindhu Garh Fort was built by Shivaji Maharaj in 1664-67 AD. According to history, he chose this rocky island to build a fort. The fort looks stunning and majestic.

Sinhagad Fort

An ideal place to go trekking, the Sinhagad Fort is about 25 km away from Pune. The Battle of Sinhagad 1670, an important war, was fought from here

Wildlife Sanctuaries

Bhimashankar Wildlife Sanctuary

Recognised as one of the biodiversity hotspots in the world, the sanctuary is home to a wide variety of species.it is among the most prominent protected habitats of the Indian Giant Squirrel.

Koyna Wildlife Sanctuary

Set amid the Western Ghats, Koyna Wildlife Sanctuary has to be on the list of wildlife sanctuary in Maharashtra. The sanctuary is a heavenly abode of an array of mammals.

Melghat Wildlife Sanctuary

With the rich biodiversity of flora and fauna, it is indeed the best wildlife sanctuary in Maharashtra. Owing to a large population of tigers, it has been declared as a tiger reserve wildlife sanctuary.
